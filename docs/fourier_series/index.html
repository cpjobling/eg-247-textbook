Fourier Series

This chapter launches our Introduction to Fourier Transforms with a look at Wave Analsysis and Trigonometric Fourier Series. We will then go on to consider the Exponential Fourier Series and its Applications.

The material in this chapter is based on Chapter 7 (Starting at Section 7.8) of (Karris, 2012) from the Required Reading List. Some clarificattion was needed and I used Chapter 4 of (missing reference) from the Recommended Reading List for this.
