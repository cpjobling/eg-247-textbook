Sine

\(\sin \omega_0 t\)

\(j\pi\left[\delta(\omega-\omega_0)-\delta(\omega+\omega_0)\right]\)

Single pole

\(e^{-at}u_0(t)\)

$\(\frac{1}{j\omega + a}\)$

\(a \gt 0\)

9.

Double pole

\(te^{-at}u_0(t)\)

$\(\frac{1}{(j\omega + a)^2}\)$

\(a\gt 0\)

10.

Complex pole (cosine component)

\(e^{-at}\cos\omega_0 t\;u_0(t)\)

$\(\frac{j\omega + a}{((j\omega + a)^2+\omega^2}\)$

\(a\gt 0\)

11.

Complex pole (sine component)

\(e^{-at}\sin \omega_0 t\;u_0(t)\)

$\(\frac{\omega}{((j\omega + a)^2+\omega^2}\)$

\(a\gt 0\)

12.

Gating function (aka \(\operatorname{rect}(T)\))

\(A\left[u_0(t + T)-u_0(t -T)\right]\)

$\(2AT\frac{\sin\omega T}{\omega T}\)$
